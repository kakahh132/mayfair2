Our Story

Founded in 2015, Mayfair Bites began as a small family-owned bakery with a passion for creating exceptional pastries. What started as a humble storefront has grown into a beloved neighborhood institution, known for our commitment to quality and craftsmanship.

Our head baker, Marie Dubois, brings over 20 years of experience from Parisian bakeries, combining traditional French techniques with local flavors. We source our ingredients carefully, using organic flour, European butter, and seasonal fruits to ensure every bite is a delight.

At Mayfair Bites, we believe baking is an art form. Each morning, our team arrives before dawn to prepare fresh batches of pastries, ensuring you always get the warmest, flakiest, most delicious treats possible.
